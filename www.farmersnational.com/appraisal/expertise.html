Expertise

Experience you can trust.

We are experts.

Our national valuation experts prepare reports in compliance with the Uniform Standards of Professional Appraisal Practice and International Valuation Standards as well as Uniform Appraisal Standards for Federal Land Acquisitions. Several team members are designated Accredited Rural Appraisers of the American Society of Farm Managers and Rural Appraisers and Members of the Appraisal Institute, or are working toward earning a designation. We are specialists who have undergone extensive training and continuing education in order to provide our clients with high quality and accurate reports that enable our clients to make the most informed decisions.

Our experience is valuable.

Our highly experienced professionals provide appraisal reports for a variety of purposes including, but not limited to, estate planning and settlement, loan and trust documentation, business reorganization, acquisition and disposition, litigation, charitable donation, exchanges, or partitioning. Our expertise allows us to consult on assignments that include feasibility studies, highest and best use studies, quantitative analytics, and real estate investment analysis to assist in decision making processes both domestically and globally.
