Grant Fitzgerald Named SVP of Farm and Ranch Management at Farmers National Company

September 25, 2025

Farmers National Company is pleased to announce the promotion of Grant Fitzgerald to Senior Vice President of Farm and Ranch Management. In his new role, Fitzgerald will lead the company’s nationwide farm management operations, continuing to drive innovation, client service, and operational excellence across the organization.

Fitzgerald brings over a decade of experience in agricultural management and has been a key leader within Farmers National Company. Most recently serving as Regional Vice President, he has played a pivotal role in expanding the company’s farm management services and strengthening relationships with landowners.

Throughout his tenure, Grant has been instrumental in advancing FARM’s operational strategy and business planning. He has led multiple teams across client services, operations, and support functions, helping to strengthen performance and collaboration. Most recently, he has led technology initiatives aimed at improving data management and streamlining internal processes.

“Grant’s deep understanding of agriculture, combined with his leadership and strategic vision, make him the ideal person to lead our farm management division into the future,” said Paul Schadegg, President of Farmers National Company. “We are confident that under his guidance, our clients will continue to receive the highest level of service and expertise.”

In his new role, Fitzgerald will oversee a team of over 100 professional farm managers across the country, focusing on delivering customized solutions that maximize land value and productivity for clients.

"I’m excited to lead a strong team that is dedicated to delivering exceptional service to America’s landowners.” Fitzgerald said. “My focus is on driving a clear strategic vision for our division, ensuring that we set the standard in our industry.”
