FAQs

Have questions? We're here to help.

  • Farmers National Company can provide an appraisal anywhere in the country. Many of our appraisers are licensed in multiple states. We have obtained temporary licenses, as needed, for a client’s project in most states in the county.

  • Farmers National Company specializes in appraising agricultural and commercial properties of all sizes and complexities. If after reviewing the clients requirements we feel that another service would offer them better results we would refer them to that service. It is our mission to deliver exceptional services through professional and trusted relationships.

  • Like all services, the time to complete an appraisal depends on the volume of work at the time the appraisal is requested. Generally, an appraisal will take from 30 to 90 days depending on work load and complexity of the project.
