This +/- 147.40-acre tract of highly productive, pivot-irrigated farmland would be an excellent addition to any operation. The farm features 122.24 certified irrigated acres, as reported by the Upper Big Blue NRD. The property has an 8-inch irrigation well with a Western Land Roller Pump. The farm is currently under a cash rent lease for the 2025 crop year. The pivot, natural gas power unit, Amarillo gearhead, and the water meter are property of the tenant and do not convey with the farm.

Property Location

From Harvard, travel 2 miles east on Road 319. The property is located at the southeast corner of the intersection of Rd N and Rd 319.
