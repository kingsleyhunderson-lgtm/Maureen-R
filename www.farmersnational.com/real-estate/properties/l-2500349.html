Cropland and pasture combination property located just 1.5 miles from Spencer. The cropland acres were planted to alfalfa in 2023, and the stand is excellent.High quality Ree, Reliance and Onita soils make up the cropland acres.

The pasture offers rural water, plus a brand new set of steel corrals!The former building site offers outstanding tree protection, rural water, and would make a perfect spot for a new home.

This property presents an outstanding opportunity to purchase high quality cropland plus pasture and a building or homesite just over one mile from Spencer, Nebraska.
