The live auction will be held on Wednesday, October 1, 2025, at 10:00 AM, at the Crooked Creek Golf Club, 333 S 134th St, Lincoln, NE 68520. Online bidding will begin on Monday, September 15, 2025, and close at the end of the live event.

Productive dryland farms between Lincoln and Eagle! These farms have the opportunity to serve as excellent row crop farm land or could be used for housing development within minutes of  84 and O St!

Property Location

Tract 1: The Northeast corner of tract 1 is at the intersection of Holdrege St. and S. 176, 1 mile north of US HWY 34 on S. 176th. 

Tract 2: From US HWY 34, turn south at S. 176th 1 mile to 'A' St., then turn west on 'A' 1/2 mile. North side of tract 2 borders 'A' St. at half-mile line. See the map for details. 

Tract 3: From US HWY 34, turn south on 176th St. 1 1/2 miles. Tract 3 is an inside 80 beginning on the south side of the half-mile line between 'A' St. and Van Dorn St. on S. 176th on the west side of the road.
