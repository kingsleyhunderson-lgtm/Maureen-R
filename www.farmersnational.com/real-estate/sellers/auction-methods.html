Public Land Auctions

The public auction process is a tried and true method of selling real estate in the United States. At Farmers National Company, we know that the real estate auction method has the ability to get you top dollar while you remain in full control of the purchase agreement, selling conditions, and terms. It also offers you a chance to sell your property in a reasonable period of time.

We work with you to choose how the actual auction will be conducted. There are several options to consider:

  • Undisclosed Reserve Auction

  • Written Bid Sales
  • Multi-Tract Auctions

Online Auctions

Farmers National Company can offer land for sale utilizing a completely online bidding system that generates more potential bidders for the property. An online auction helps attract the highest bidder into the competition to buy the auction property as it allows people to safely bid from anywhere in the world. Farmers National Company’s extensive marketing of each land auction sale coupled with the opportunity to utilize online bidding will successfully generate more bidders and increased competition when selling your land. We help manage the closing process on the auction day to ensure a secure and reliable transaction.



Available Online Land Auction Methods:

  • Simulcast Auctions

    • Live Public Auction

    • Online Only Auction

      • The auction is conducted through our online platform only with a set starting and ending date

    • Virtual Online Auction

      • The auction is conducted by a live auctioneer in a virtual private setting with bids taken live through our online platform.

    • Simulcast Live and Online Auction

      • This auction method combines our live public and online methods allowing potential buyers to submit bids both live in person or through the online platform in real time.

    • For Sale by Bid

      • The For Sale by Bid process allows the agent to market the property through normal channels then accept bids during a set timeline. Bids are then shared with the Seller to determine the best and highest offer to consider.

    • The ideal timeframe is 45 to 60 days ahead of the scheduled auction date to properly market and expose the property to the open market. We typically allow 30 days following the auction date to complete and close the transaction.
