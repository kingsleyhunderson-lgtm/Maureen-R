Mineral Assets

Expert Asset Management Services

We work behind the scenes monitoring your mineral ownership to maximize your revenue.

  • We identify, catalog, and organize all of your mineral assets. This ensures reporting is accurate and concise, allowing us to clearly outline the status of all of your assets.

  • Our GIS Mapping creates visuals of your mineral assets and showcases nearby leasing and permitting, which can potentially impact oil and gas specialty assets. With this information, we guide you to make informed decisions on potential opportunities and mitigate risks.

  • With our client portal, you can quickly access monthly performance, annual statements, and other essential reporting information on your mineral asset management.

Mineral Finances

Financial Oversight

Your finances are in expert hands when you choose FNC Energy to manage your mineral assets.

  • Our team collects and distributes your mineral revenue, providing accurate and concise reporting to keep you up-to-date and informed.

  • Failure to pay certain mineral taxes can negatively impact your mineral assets and revenue. We ensure your tax payments are paid on time and protest any taxes that are assessed too highly.

  • Incorrect information on division order forms may delay production payments. We verify and update division order information for you, ensuring all information is accurate and submitted in a timely manner.

Protecting Investment and Financial Services’ Mineral Finances

We protect and guide financial institutions to make smart decisions about their mineral rights.
