Our Solution

For Risk Mitgation

FNC Energy is dedicated to risk mitigation mineral services that ensure your mineral assets are protected. Contact us today to learn how our services can reduce risk and provide support for your mineral management needs.

  • One of the biggest challenges mineral owners face is recovering funds in suspense of preventing escheatment. Our team conducts thorough due diligence to identify funds in suspense and pursue them quickly. We also actively prevent escheatment by maintaining compliance with state regulations and taking swift action to recover lost funds. Our mineral risk management helps make sure companies responsible for mineral exploration are safeguarded against potential revenue loss.

  • We handle dormant mineral filings to secure your mineral ownership rights and protect your mineral resources from being claimed by others. Our management team files the necessary documents to maintain your rights and helps business owners and individuals stay compliant to avoid costly disputes.

  • Title defects have the potential to disrupt your revenue stream. We specialize in curing these titles using our expertise in managing title resolutions to keep your operations up and running. We address risks proactively to provide peace of mind for our mineral owners.

  • Our approach to risk mitigation mineral management includes a thorough analysis of your assets and implements customized strategies. We respond to challenges within the industry and focus on future performance and efficiency. From maintaining compliance to protecting against unforeseen challenges and other risks, our methods help mitigate risks and keep your mineral ownership secure.
